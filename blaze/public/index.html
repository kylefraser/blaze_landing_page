Blaze is...

A comprehensive app connecting outdoor enthusiasts, trail hikers and land management organizations. Blaze responsibly. We are outdoorsy folk who want to preserve trails and make your hikes better. Improve trail conditions, report real time weather updates, and inspire new adventures. Post your real time updates within app or through Facebook. Meet each other virtually on the trail to share your experience. Give your community real time updates, track trail obstacles and improve conditions together.

Pay it forward. Help other hikers make the most of their adventures. Report real time weather updates, trail conditions and incredible outdoor opportunities with other hikers and preservation organizations.
